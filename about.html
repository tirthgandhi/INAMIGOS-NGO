About InAmigos Foundation

Founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla — a Section 8 non-profit licensed by the Central Government, based in Chhattisgarh.

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government, with its base in Chhattisgarh. The foundation holds 80G & 12A certifications, ensuring transparency, accountability, and tax-exempt benefits for donors. We are CSR-1 registered for corporate partnerships, NGO DARPAN registered with NITI Aayog, and hold IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification for quality in operations and service delivery.

With a strong network of dedicated professionals, volunteers, and corporate partners, we work tirelessly to bring positive change through food distribution, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and social inclusion programs across India. Our social media platforms actively showcase our impact, keeping supporters informed and engaged.

Our Mission

To mobilize collective action that directs every contribution toward essential causes — nourishing communities, educating children, empowering women, protecting animals, restoring the environment, and building employable youth across India.

Our Vision

To create a more inclusive, compassionate, and empowered society where every individual has access to dignity, opportunity, and the support needed to thrive.

Social Impact

We measure success in lives reached — from 50,000+ beneficiaries and 1 lakh+ interns trained to 20,000+ trees planted and daily animal care — turning purpose into measurable community transformation.
