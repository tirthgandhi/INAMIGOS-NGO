Project Bachpanshala

Education Opens Doors in Rural Chhattisgarh

A child who could not attend school due to poverty received books and volunteer teaching through Project Bachpanshala. Today, with sustained mentorship, she is pursuing her dream of becoming a teacher and helping others in her village learn to read.

"InAmigos gave me hope when I had none. Now I want to give others that same opportunity."
0 Children Helped
Project Seva

Meals That Restored Dignity

During a Project Seva food drive in a low-income neighbourhood, families received nutritious meals and essential supplies. Volunteers documented how consistent support helped parents focus on work while children returned to school with full stomachs and renewed confidence.

"When someone cares enough to feed your family, you find strength to rebuild."
0 Meals Distributed
Project Udaan

Digital Skills, Independent Futures

Through Project Udaan's digital literacy program, a young woman learned online business skills and now earns a steady income supporting her family — demonstrating how women empowerment creates ripple effects across entire households.

"Digital literacy changed my life. Now I am independent and confident."
0 Girls Empowered
Project Prakriti

From Wasteland to Green Community

Project Prakriti's tree plantation initiative transformed degraded land in a Maharashtra village. Local residents joined cleanliness drives and now maintain green spaces that improve air quality and create livelihood opportunities.

"Our village now has clean air and income from the trees we planted together."
0 Trees Planted
Project Jeev

Compassion for Every Life

Project Jeev volunteers feed and care for stray animals daily. Community members now report injured animals for rescue, reflecting a cultural shift toward empathy nurtured through consistent welfare work.

"Every creature deserves care — Jeev taught our children that lesson."
0 Animals Fed Daily
Project Vikas

Internship to Employment

A Project Vikas intern gained hands-on experience in community management and social work. The ISO-certified certificate and mentorship led to a full-time role — one of 250+ interns who joined the InAmigos team permanently.

"Vikas was not just an internship — it was my pathway to meaningful work."
